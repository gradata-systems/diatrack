Getting Started

To get started, let's connect to your Dexcom continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) account.

This will enable Diatrack to continuously download your CGM data, so it's available to you whenever you're using this app.

Connect to a datasource

See the video below for a walkthrough of how to connect to your CGM provider (currently only Dexcom G5/G6 are supported).

Note the Geographic region setting. This is important and you will not be able to connect successfully if you choose the wrong one. If you're in the United States, select the US option. Otherwise your region will be EU.
